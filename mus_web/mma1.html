BTS's initial years at Melon Music Awards saw them garnering attention and nominations, reflecting their emergence as a promising force in the Korean music industry. As BTS's popularity surged domestically and internationally, their presence at the Melon Music Awards became more pronounced. Nominations in various categories showcased the group's versatility and widespread appeal, setting the stage for their future dominance.
