Jung Ho-seok

J-Hope, whose real name is Jung Hoseok, is a vibrant and talented member of the globally acclaimed boy band BTS. Born on February 18, 1994, in Gwangju, South Korea, J-Hope brings an infectious energy and charisma to the group.

Known for his exceptional dance skills and bright personality, J-Hope plays a pivotal role as the main dancer and rapper in BTS. His stage name reflects his aspiration to be a source of hope and positivity for fans around the world.

Beyond his contributions to the group's dynamic performances, J-Hope has showcased his versatility as a solo artist. His mixtape, "Hope World," released in 2018, received widespread acclaim for its diverse musical styles and reflective lyrics.

J-Hope's journey is characterized by his unwavering passion for music and dance, and his uplifting presence has made him a beloved figure among BTS fans. With his unique blend of talent and optimism, J-Hope continues to leave an indelible mark on the global music scene.
