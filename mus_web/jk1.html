Jeon Jung-kook

Jungkook, widely known as JK, is a multifaceted artist and the youngest member of the global sensation BTS.

As the main vocalist, lead dancer, and youngest member, JK has become known for his powerful vocals, exceptional dance skills, and charismatic stage presence. Starting his journey in the entertainment industry at a young age, Jungkook has evolved into a versatile artist, contributing to BTS's global success. Beyond music, his genuine personality and dedication to self-improvement have endeared him to fans worldwide. With a bright future ahead, Jungkook continues to inspire as a key figure in the dynamic world of K-pop.

Jeon Jung-kook childhood

Birthplace: Jungkook, whose full name is Jeon Jungkook, was born on September 1, 1997, in Busan, South Korea, not Daegu.

Nickname: Jungkook is often affectionately called "JK" by fans, which is a shortened version of his name and a play on "Jungkook."

Family: Specific details about Jungkook's family are not extensively publicized, as BTS members generally maintain a level of privacy regarding their personal lives.

Interest in Arts: Jungkook exhibited a passion for the arts from an early age. He displayed remarkable talent in singing and dancing, which ultimately led him to pursue a career in the entertainment industry. His early interest in the performing arts played a significant role in his development as a versatile performer.

Pre-BTS Years: Before joining BTS, Jungkook gained recognition for his exceptional dance skills and vocal abilities. He participated in various local dance competitions and caught the attention of entertainment agencies. His journey to becoming an idol began when he auditioned for "Superstar K" at the age of 13, ultimately leading him to join Big Hit Entertainment as a trainee.

Education: Jungkook attended Baek Yang Middle School in Busan and later transferred to Singu Middle School in Seoul. His passion for the arts and his decision to pursue a career in the entertainment industry influenced his educational path as he embarked on his journey with BTS.

Jungkook's childhood and formative years reflect his early dedication to the performing arts, paving the way for his success as a member of BTS.

Jeon Jung-kook Milatary Service

Jungkook of BTS is starting South Korea's mandatory military service next month.

On Wednesday, Jungkook announced he'll begin his time in the military in December via a statement on WeVerse, and BTS' record label BigHit Music revealed the "Seven" singer have each begun the process of enlistment.

"In December, I will start a new journey. I'm leaving you for a short while to serve in the military," wrote Jungkook, 26, on WeVerse in a post translated from Korean to English. "As I share this news, I feel heavy on one hand, and on the other hand, I'm reminded of precious memories with ARMY, so my heart warms up."
