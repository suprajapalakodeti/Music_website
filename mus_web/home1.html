Overview of HYBE Corporation

Founding and History:

HYBE Corporation was founded in 2005 by Bang Si-Hyuk, a prominent figure in the music industry.

Initially known as Big Hit Entertainment, the company started as a small agency with a focus on music production and artist development.

K-pop Sensation BTS:

One of the major milestones for HYBE Corporation was the formation and success of the boy band BTS (Bangtan Sonyeondan). BTS debuted in 2013 and quickly gained international acclaim for their music, performances, and socially conscious messages.

Expansion and Rebranding:

As part of its growth strategy, Big Hit Entertainment underwent a rebranding in 2021, changing its name to HYBE Corporation. This move reflected the company's broader scope and diversification into various entertainment sectors beyond music.

Diversification of Business:

HYBE has expanded its business interests to include various entertainment divisions such as music production, artist management, concert production, and content creation.

The company has also ventured into other areas, including mobile apps, gaming, and multimedia content development.

Strategic Partnerships:

HYBE Corporation has engaged in strategic partnerships with other entertainment companies and entities to further its global presence.

The company's collaborations and joint ventures have aimed at exploring new opportunities in the entertainment industry.

Philanthropy and Social Initiatives:

Like its predecessor Big Hit Entertainment, HYBE Corporation has been involved in philanthropic initiatives. This includes social responsibility campaigns and charitable contributions to various causes.

Global Impact:

HYBE's global impact extends beyond music to influence fashion, lifestyle, and popular culture. The company's artists, particularly BTS, have played a significant role in popularizing K-pop worldwide.
